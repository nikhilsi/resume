## About

I build. That has been the constant across 25+ years, whether it was stabilizing a broken platform at Expedia, scaling a PE-backed startup from $1M to $4M ARR, or leading 200+ engineers across Fortune 500 engagements.

Ten months ago, I started building differently. AI became my primary development partner. What started as one project became fourteen repos and nearly 3,000 commits. I shipped 8 products — web apps, iOS and Android apps, Chrome and VS Code extensions, Electron tools, a multi-model AI platform — all built daily with Claude Code, across React, Next.js, FastAPI, Express+TypeScript, SwiftUI, Electron, and Python.

The building taught me something: the gap between what a human means and what an AI does is not just a product problem. It is a governance problem. If you cannot structurally express intent, you cannot govern what happens next. So I started writing about it and contributing to federal AI standards. I did not plan to become a governance voice. The building made it unavoidable.

## What I'm Building

### [Recurate Extensions](https://github.com/nikhilsi/recurate) · [recurate.ai](https://recurate.ai)
Chrome + VS Code extensions for annotating AI responses. Highlight, strikethrough, dig deeper, verify — structured feedback that auto-injects into your next message. Works on claude.ai, ChatGPT, and Microsoft Copilot (both Enterprise and Consumer). Published on Chrome Web Store, VS Code Marketplace, and Open VSX.

### [Recurate Platform](https://app.recurate.ai)
Multi-model AI comparison with a curation loop. Ask a question once, get responses from Claude, GPT, Gemini, and Grok side by side. Keep the best parts, drop the rest, continue the conversation. Multi-turn. The models are the commodity. The curation is what's missing everywhere else. FastAPI, React, multi-model API integration.

### [ScreenTrades](https://screentrades.ai)
AI-powered trading analysis. 1,882 commits over 10 months — the origin project where the AI partnership started. Aggregates 20+ real-time data APIs, delivers analysis with confidence scores, entry/exit points, transparent reasoning. V2.0 web + iOS V2 shipped. React, FastAPI, Claude AI.

### [TourGraph](https://github.com/nikhilsi/tourgraph) · [tourgraph.ai](https://tourgraph.ai)
Tour discovery across 136K+ experiences in 3,380 destinations. Four features: Tour Roulette, Right Now Somewhere, The World's Most, Six Degrees of Anywhere. v10.0.0: standalone Express 5 + TypeScript backend API, Next.js web, SwiftUI iOS with MapKit globe + 2,694 destination pins.

### [GitaVani](https://github.com/nikhilsi/gitavani) · [gitavani.app](https://gitavani.app)
Bhagavad Gita reader. 701 verses, 17 scholars, Sanskrit audio, 4 visual themes. iOS (App Store) + Android. Swift/SwiftUI, zero external dependencies, fully offline.

### [ClearNews](https://github.com/nikhilsi/news-aggregator) · [getclearnews.com](https://getclearnews.com)
News aggregator. 41 sources, 13 categories, in-app reader view. No ads, no clickbait. iOS (App Store) + Android + Web + API. Next.js, SwiftUI, FastAPI.

### [CoPilots Launchpad](https://github.com/nikhilsi/copilots-launchpad)
Electron desktop app for M365 test account management. Playwright auto-login, isolated browser profiles, encrypted credentials via OS keychain. Azure Trusted Signing for Windows. macOS + Windows. MIT license. Idea to shipped in 4 hours.

## What the Building Taught Me

### [From Instinct to Intent™](https://aitrustcommons.org/blog/)
A publication series on the missing interface between human judgment and machine execution. The concept: between what a human means and what AI does, there needs to be a structural layer — not natural language, not prompt engineering, but enforceable constraint declarations. USPTO trademark filed ([Serial 99690685](https://tsdr.uspto.gov/#caseNumber=99690685&caseSearchType=US_APPLICATION&caseType=DEFAULT&searchType=statusSearch)). Book in progress.

- **Article 1:** [Discovering Intent: The Journey That Starts Before You're Ready](https://nikhilsinghal-ai-trust-commons.medium.com/discovering-intent-the-journey-that-starts-before-youre-ready-4a8a69fce594) — The manifesto. Published on [Zenodo](https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.18917473), [Medium](https://nikhilsinghal-ai-trust-commons.medium.com/discovering-intent-the-journey-that-starts-before-youre-ready-4a8a69fce594), and [aitrustcommons.org](https://aitrustcommons.org/blog/2026/03/08/discovering-intent/).
- **Article 2:** [We Are Making AI Write Code in Languages Designed for Humans. That Is the Problem.](https://medium.com/@nikhilsinghal-ai-trust-commons/we-are-making-ai-write-code-in-languages-designed-for-humans-that-is-the-problem-d6721ab296ad) — AI-native programming languages and the Intent Layer. Published on [Zenodo](https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.19005877), [Medium](https://medium.com/@nikhilsinghal-ai-trust-commons/we-are-making-ai-write-code-in-languages-designed-for-humans-that-is-the-problem-d6721ab296ad), and [aitrustcommons.org](https://aitrustcommons.org/blog/2026/03/13/languages-designed-for-humans/).

### [NIST Public Comment on AI Agent Governance](https://www.regulations.gov/comment/NIST-2025-0035-0352)
~5,000 word submission to NIST CAISI docket on AI agent security. Three arguments: organizational governance is already broken and agents make it worse, NIST should drive convergence across existing standards rather than adding another layer, and compliance must become machine-readable. Grounded in deploying compliance across 8 organizations, 7 industries, and regimes including SOC 2, PCI DSS, HIPAA, COPPA, FERPA, FCC, and ITAR/EAR.

### [AI Trust Commons](https://aitrustcommons.org) · [GitHub org](https://github.com/aitrustcommons)
Open-source governance for AI agents in the enterprise. Research, standards alignment, and the From Instinct to Intent™ publication series.
